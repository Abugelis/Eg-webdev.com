Why Your Website is Losing Clients

Your website should attract clients — not scare them away.

Most law firms unknowingly lose potential clients because of these common issues:

Outdated Design

First impressions matter.

A website that looks like it’s from 2010 makes clients question your professionalism.

Slow Load Times

Every second counts.

If your site takes more than 3 seconds to load, visitors leave — and you never see them again.

Not Mobile-Friendly

Most clients browse on their phones.

If your site isn’t optimized for mobile, you’re literally losing half your audience.

Hard-to-Find Information

Clients want clarity.

If your services, contact info, or office location are buried, they’ll pick another firm instead.
